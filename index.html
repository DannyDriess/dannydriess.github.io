Danny Driess

I am a PhD student in Machine Learning and Robotics since 2019, affiliated with the TU Berlin, SCIoI and the IMPRS-IS.
My advisor is Marc Toussaint.
In 2020, I visited Russ Tedrake's Lab at MIT.
Currently, I am a Student Researcher at Google Brain, working with Pete Florence.
I organized three RSS workshops in 2020, 2021 discussing the synergies between learning and planning, and 2022 on Implicit Representations for Robotics.
